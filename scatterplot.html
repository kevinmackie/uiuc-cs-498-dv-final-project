Research on SCADA Cybersecurity

About the Visualization

This website will teach you about and give you an opportunity to explore the research literature on SCADA cybersecurity. SCADA systems are centralized monitoring and systems for pipelines, electrical grids, etc.

In the 80s and 90s there is relatively little cybersecurity research.

But post 9-11 there is an abrupt increase in the amount of research.
