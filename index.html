SCADA Cybersecurity Research Landscape

This interactive website will present the research landscape for SCADA cybersecurity, after which it will allow you to explore this research yourself via an interactive webpage.

SCADA systems are computerized control systems for critical infrastructure networks such as oil and gas pipelines; water, electrical, or gas distribution systems, etc.

The graph on the right shows statistics of SCADA cybersecurity research published prior to 2002.

See the next slide for research trends after 2001.

Post 9-11 Increase in Research

The trend shows an abrupt increase in SCADA cybersecurity reserach following the attacks of 9-11. Correlation does not imply causation, and more detailed research would be needed to know what effect the terrorists attacks had on the volume of security research.

Research Citations

We see here the total citations to date for papers published in each year. The general ratio is approximately 10-20 citations per paper. The drop-off in citations after 2012 suggests that it takes more than 5 years for the full effect of research to be felt.

We also see an interesting jump in research citations in the 2010-2011 timeframe.
